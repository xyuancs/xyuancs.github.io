### Overview

I am a Lecturer in the [Faculty of Information Technology](https://www.monash.edu/it) at [Monash University](https://www.monash.edu/it/about-us/our-rankings), and affiliated with the [Monash Cybersecurity Lab](http://www.monash.edu/cybersecurity-lab) and the [Monash Blockchain Technology Centre](https://www.monash.edu/blockchain). I earned my PhD degree from [City University of Hong Kong](http://www.cityu.edu.hk/) in 2016.

<br />
My research focuses on addressing the data privacy and security issues in cloud and networked systems. I am also interested in inter-disciplinary research with digital health, Internet of things, building information modelling, and machine learning. My research has been supported by Australian Research Council, Monash Infrastructure, Monash Faculty of IT, CSIRO Data61, Oceania Cyber Security Centre, Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, Ansen Innovation, and Collinstar Capital.

<br />
I am always looking for outstanding students; please email me your CV and supporting documents (e.g., transcripts, research statement, english test scores). More details on PhD applications can be found [here](http://www.monash.edu/graduate-research/future-students/apply), [here](https://www.monash.edu/graduate-research/future-students/support/international-students/china-scholarship-council), and [here](https://xyuancs.github.io/PhD-adv.md).
