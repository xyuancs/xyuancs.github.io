Biography

I am currently a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Software Systems and Cybersecurity of the Faculty of Information Technology at Monash University. I am also a Deputy Course Director for Master of Cybersecurity at Monash. I joined Monash University in 2017 after I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong. I obtained my BS degree and MS degree both in Electrical Engineering from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications and Illinois Institute of Technology, respectively. Prior to that, I received my secondary education from High School Affiliated To Nanjing Normal University. From 2011 to 2013, I worked at Stromasys S.A., developing and optimising the dynamic instruction translation engine for virtual SPARC and ALPHA servers on the x86 platform.

Research Interests

I have broad interests in cybersecurity, including data security and privacy, secure networked system, trustworthy machine learning, and confidential computing. My recent research focus can be found here.
