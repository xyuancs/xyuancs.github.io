Overview

I am a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Software Systems and Cybersecurity of the Faculty of Information Technology, and also the Course Director for Master of Cybersecurity at Monash University. I joined Monash since 2017 after I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong. I have broad interests in cybersecurity, including data security and privacy, secure networked system, trustworthy machine learning, and confidential computing. My recent research focus can be found here. I am always looking for self-motivated students. Please email me your CV, transcript, research statement, english test score. Information on Monash PhD admission and scholarships can be found here, here, and here.
